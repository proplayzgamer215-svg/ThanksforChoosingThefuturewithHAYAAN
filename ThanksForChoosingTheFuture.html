About Me

My name is Hayaan, and I am a 10-year-old kid who has a strong passion for coding, technology, and learning how things work. I love to code and enjoy creating programs, experimenting with ideas, and solving problems using logic and creativity. I am especially interested in electronics and am known as a circuit expert for my age because I enjoy designing, understanding, and building circuits using different components and connections. I often make circuits using Arduino, where I connect sensors, LEDs, buzzers, and other components to create real working projects. I enjoy writing Arduino code, testing circuits, and fixing problems until everything works correctly. Along with coding and circuits, I am also a big aviation enthusiast, also known as an avgeek, and I know a lot about airplanes and aviation technology. I enjoy learning about different types of aircraft, how planes fly, jet engines, airlines, airports, and the science behind aviation. I am always curious about how modern planes are designed and how technology makes air travel safer and more efficient. I love learning new things every day, whether it is through coding, building Arduino circuits, experimenting with electronics, or learning about aviation, and I enjoy challenging myself with new projects that help me improve my skills step by step. I believe learning should be fun, hands-on, and exciting, and I am always motivated to keep exploring, building, and growing my knowledge.
